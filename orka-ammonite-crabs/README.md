# Orka Ammonite Crabs

Combat training at Ammonite Crabs on Fossil Island with optional Birdhouse Runs.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🎯 **AFK Training:** Automatic aggro resetting.
- ⚔️ **Combat:** Supports all combat styles and special attacks.
- 🏦 **Banking:** Fossil Island or Crafting Guild.
- 🐦 **Birdhouse Runs:** Optional integrated birdhouse runs every ~50 minutes.
- 💎 **Looting:** Optional looting for Fossils (Small, Medium, Large, Rare) and Easy Clue Scrolls.
- 🧪 **Consumables:** Supports food and potions (Combat, Super Combat, Ranging, etc.).
- 🤖 **Antiban:** Includes breaks, sleeps, and human-like behavior.

---

## 📋 Requirements

- **Quest:** Bone Voyage completed (required for Fossil Island access).
- **Digsite Pendants:** Required if Birdhouse Runs are enabled or if banking at the Crafting Guild.

---

## ⚙️ Setup

### 📍 Starting Position

- **Fossil Island bank:** Start your character on Fossil Island. It will automatically analyze if it needs to bank first.
- **Crafting Guild bank:** Anywhere, as long as you have a Crafting Cape equipped or in inventory (it will teleport to the Crafting Guild automatically). You will also need Digsite Pendants in bank to teleport back to Fossil Island.

### 🏦 Bank Setup

- Place required items in the **first bank tab, at the top** (Food, Potions, Digsite Pendants, etc.).
- The script searches for missing items but this is slower.

### 🐦 Birdhouse Requirements (If Enabled)

- 1x Clockwork (optional, speeds up run)
- Stack of desired logs
- Stack of birdhouse seeds (Potato by default)
- 1x Hammer, 1x Chisel
- 2x Digsite Pendant spots (1 low-charge, 1 full stack) — Have your charged jewelry with the low charged one to the left and the stack of fully charged jewelry to the right.

Any ideas or feedback will be greatly appreciated.
