# Orka Reanimation

Efficiently reanimates ensouled heads at the Dark Altar in Arceuus.
The cheapest prayer training method with great xp/h rates (up to 200k+ xp/h with dragon heads).

---

## 📋 Requirements

### 🔮 Magic & Spells

- **Spellbook:** Arceuus
- **Magic Levels:**
  - ✨ **Lvl 16** (Basic): Goblin, Monkey, Imp, Minotaur, Scorpion, Bear, Unicorn
  - ✨ **Lvl 41** (Adept): Dog, Druid, Ogre, Giant, Elf, Troll, Horror
  - ✨ **Lvl 72** (Expert): Kalphite, Dagannoth, Bloodveld, TzHaar, Demon, Hellhound
  - ✨ **Lvl 90** (Master): Aviansie, Abyssal, Dragon

### 🎒 Runes

- Supports **Rune Pouch** & **Divine Rune Pouch**
- Have required Nature, Body, Soul, or Blood runes in inventory/rune pouch.

### ⚔️ Gear & Combat

- **Equipment:** Wear your best melee gear. Quicker kills = Higher XP/h!
- Must start script with gear equipped.
- **Safety:** ⚠️ No food support. For the best xp rates you must be able to kill a full inventory of heads comfortably.
- **Failsafe:** Script teleports back to Ferox Enclave if health goes below 40%.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- 🏰 **Ferox Enclave Banking:** Restores stats at the Pool of Refreshment every trip.
- ⚔️ **Weapon Specs:** Uses special attacks to speed up kills.
- 🛡️ **Quick Prayers:** Intelligent usage of your pre-configured quick prayers.
- 🧪 **1-Dose Potions:** Supports sipping 1-dose Combat/Antifire potions at the bank. *(Tip: Use Bob Barter at GE to decant potions to 1-dose.)*
- 🐦 **Birdhouse Runs:** Fully integrated Birdhouse run support.

---

## ⚙️ Setup Checklist

- **Start Location:** 📍 Ferox Enclave (Recommended) or Arceuus Altar area with Ring of Dueling equipped.
- **Inventory:** Start with Runes + Ring of Dueling equipped.
- **Bank Setup:** Place Heads, Rings, and Potions in the Main Tab for speed.
- **Dragons (optional):** If doing Dragon heads, have Antifire(1) or Extended Antifire(1) in bank.

### 🐦 Birdhouse Requirements (If Enabled)

- Clockwork, Hammer, Chisel
- Logs & Seeds (Stacks)
- 2x Digsite Pendants: 1 low-charge for usage, 1 full stack in bank
