# Orka Lead Miner

Mines lead ore on Pandemonium Island with automatic banking and cave navigation.

---

## 📋 Requirements

- **Mining:** Level 25 or higher.
- **Quest:** Pandemonium quest completed (required for Pandemonium Island access).
- **XP Bar:** Must be visible and tracking Mining XP (script checks XP gain on startup).

---

## ⚙️ Setup

### 📍 Starting Position

You can start the script in any of these places:

- Near the Pandemonium Island bank.
- Near the lead ore rocks inside the Pandemonium cave.

### 🔄 Script Behavior

The script will:

- Bank on Pandemonium Island.
- Enter and exit the cave automatically.
- Walk between bank and lead rocks.
- Mine lead until the inventory is full, then bank and repeat.

### ✅ Before Starting

- Equip your pickaxe (Dragon Pickaxe spec is supported if detected).
- Make sure your character is on Pandemonium Island or already inside the Pandemonium cave.
- Open your XP bar and confirm it shows your Mining XP.
