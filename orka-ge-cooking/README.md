# Orka GE Cooking

Train cooking 1-99 at the Grand Exchange! Can be profitable depending on food prices.

---

## ✅ Requirements

- **Location:** Grand Exchange
- **Bank:** Raw food of your choice
- **Optional:** Tinderbox + Logs (for backup fire creation)

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- 🔥 **Forester Campfires:** Uses existing campfires for the best XP rates
- 🔄 **Auto GE Switching:** Auto-detects and switches GE sides
- 🪵 **Backup Fires:** Creates own fires as backup if needed
- 🔍 **Smart Fire Scanning:** Intelligent fire detection & recovery
- 🍖 **12+ Food Types:** Wide variety of supported foods
- 🛡️ **Full Antiban & Break System**

---

## 💡 Tips

- Use **populated worlds**! Campfires last indefinitely when players feed them with logs
- Start near a **Forester's Campfire** for best results. XP rates are considerably higher with campfires vs self-made fires
- Script auto-creates fires as backup, but **campfire mode is optimal**
- Keep **tinderbox + logs** in bank just in case
